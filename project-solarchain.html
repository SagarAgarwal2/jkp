Project Overview

Solar-Chain is a decentralized energy trading platform built on the Algorand blockchain that enables peer-to-peer solar energy transactions. The project reached Round 3 of the Algorand hackathon and was awarded $250 for its innovative approach to sustainable energy distribution.

The platform addresses the critical challenge of coal dependency by creating a transparent marketplace where solar energy producers can directly sell excess power to consumers. By eliminating intermediaries and reducing reliance on traditional coal-powered grids, Solar-Chain has the potential to reduce coal usage by 40–50% in participating communities.

Built on Algorand for its fast transaction speeds and low fees, the platform uses smart contracts to automate energy trading, pricing, and settlement. The system ensures:

  • Trustless Transactions: Smart contracts handle all trades without intermediaries
  • Real-time Pricing: Dynamic pricing based on supply and demand
  • Carbon Credits: Automatic tracking and issuance of carbon offset credits
  • Micropayments: Algorand's low fees enable small-scale energy trades

Key Features

  • P2P Energy Marketplace: Direct trading between solar producers and consumers
  • Smart Contract Automation: Automated execution of trades based on predefined conditions
  • Real-time Monitoring: Live dashboard showing energy production, consumption, and trading activity
  • Dynamic Pricing Algorithm: Market-driven pricing that balances supply and demand
  • Carbon Tracking: Transparent measurement of carbon footprint reduction
  • Wallet Integration: Seamless connection with Algorand wallets for transactions
  • Energy Credits: Tokenized representation of solar energy units
  • Settlement System: Instant settlement of energy trades on the blockchain

Technical Architecture

The platform consists of three main components:

  • Smart Contracts (PyTeal): Handle energy trading logic, pricing, and settlement on Algorand
  • Backend (Python): Manages IoT data from solar panels and energy meters
  • Frontend (React): User interface for producers and consumers to trade energy

The system integrates with IoT devices to monitor real-time energy production and consumption, automatically updating the blockchain with accurate data for transparent trading.
